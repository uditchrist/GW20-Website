FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


November 28th & 29th      10 Events      Gateways-2020

Register Now

Who can register?

Students who are pursuing PG Computer Science Courses like MCA, MSc. CS, MSc. Data Science, Msc. Data Analytics, MSc. IT, MS. CS, MTech CS, etc.

What is the registration process?

From the Gateways website click on the registration link from the navigation bar, fill in the basic details, after which you will be asked to pay the registration amount through CHRIST (Deemed to be University) payment gateway. After the payment, the participant has to upload the screenshot in the user’s dashboard. The payment confirmation will be intimated through EMAIL and a notification in the user’s dashboard will be provided in the website, after which you can register for different events. Note: Participants who are participating ONLY for The Grand Hackathon event do not need to register or pay the amount in the Gateways website as the event is free. They have to register individually and as a team in DEVFOLIO website..

How much is the registration fee?

The participation fee is Rs. 150 for each individual.

Is it individual registration?

Yes, participants have to register and pay individually.

What has to be done after registration is successful?

After registration, a mail will be sent to the participant stating if the registration is successful or not. If the registration is successful individual payment must be made. Once the payment is successful, the participants can register for the events from their respective dashboard.

What has to be done if the registration is rejected?

If the participant feels the reason for rejection is not genuine, then contact any Gateways Coordinator regarding the same.

How to login?

In the Gateways website, there is a login option in the navigation menu, click on it and it will take you to the login page. Provide your credentials and click on the login button to login into your dashboard.

I forgot my password?

Contact Gateways General co-ordinator for getting the password.

Where can I see my profile ?

After you login into your dashboard, On top right corner your name would be visible, click that and you will find My Profile, click that.

How much should I pay?

The participation fee is Rs. 150 for each individual. Note if the participant is ONLY to participate in THE GRAND HACKATHON, he/she is not having a registration amount, But if the same participant needs to participate in an other event, he/she have to register in GATEWAYS Website and pay the registration amount.

What is the payment process?

After registration is successful a mail will be sent to the participant for payment. The payment has to be made through CHRIST (Deemed to be University) payment gateway.

What are the payment options?

There is only one mode (ONLINE) thorugh CHRIST (Deemed to be University) Website Payment Portal.

Can I get a refund?

There is no refund option available.

What to do if payment is unsuccessful?

Try again with a different CARD or after sometime.

What has to be done if payment is successful?

After payment is successful a mail will be sent to the participant within 2 days of the payment and will also be notified through the participant dashboard. The participant can register upon successful payment.

Should I pay for each event?

No, The participants need not have to pay for each event. The participants should pay Rs. 150 individually and can take part in any event they are interested in.

How many events are there?

There are totally 10 events, 6 technical and 4 non-technical events.

Where can I get event details?

The event details are available in the websites events tab in navigation bar.

Where can I view the events that I have registered?

You can view the events that you have registered in users dashboard.

In which platforms will the events be conducted?

The events can be conducted in Google Meet, Cisco Webex or Microsoft Teams.

Does the participants get a participation certificate ?

E-Certificate will be provided to all the participants through their mail.

Can I participate again in case of network issues ?

This decision depends on the event coordinators. You can request the event coordinator for the same.

What if I’m not able to take part in the event on time ?

Late Comers are not entertained, but the decision of the event coordinator is final.

Whom should I contact to get more details of the event?

Kindly contact the respective event coordinator for more details.

When will the winners be announced?

Winners will be announced on the 2nd day of the event.

What are the prizes for each event?

Cash Prizes will be provided for the winners based on the events.

How will I receive a prize if I win ?

For Hackathon, Devfolio team will contact the winners personally. And for the rest of the events, it will be announced on the valedictory ceremony of the event.
